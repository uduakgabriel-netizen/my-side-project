    The search for nutritious dishes is very real and that gave birth to this vegetable rice dish. I look for new creative ways to infuse vegetables into my everyday meals because of their high vitamin and mineral content daily. The older you get, the more vitamins you need to function and even look healthy.

    We have several variations of rice dishes like Jollof rice, Fried Rice, Coconut Rice, Concoction rice which I have not yet made and so I figured why not add one more to our culinary repertoire? It is very quick to make and unbelievably tasty. I used spinach as my source of green vegetables but you can use the green vegetables available to you.

    Prep time: 15 mins

    Cook time: 45 mins

    How to make Vegetable Rice

    We have several variations of rice dishes like Jollof rice, Fried Rice, Coconut Rice, Concoction rice which I have not yet made and so I figured why not add one more to our culinary repertoire? It is very quick to make and unbelievably tasty. I used spinach as my source of green vegetables but you can use the green vegetables available to you.

    Ingredients

    • 4 cups Parboiled rice washed
    • 1 medium White onion
    • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
    • 2 cups of chopped green vegetables (Spinach or Ugwu)
    • 1.5 Tablespoons of melted butter
    • Spring onions chopped
    • Seasoning cubes
    • 1 teaspoon of Chili powder
    • palm oil
    • 2 Chicken thighs chopped and seasoned with salt, pepper, chilli powder, soy sauce and buillion
    • 4 cups Chicken stock can be homemade or store bought

    Instructions

    Preparing the rice;

    1. Wash and bring to boil your rice for about 10 minutes.
    2. Chop your green vegetables and set aside. Strain the water from the rice and reduce the heat. Add your vegetables, butter, lemon juice and chili powder.
    3. Chop your garlic and add to the pot of rice with your seasoning cubes. Allow steaming on low heat till the rice is soft.
    4. Serve your vegetable rice hot with any protein of your choice.
