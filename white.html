    • White Soup Recipe

    White soup is a brightly flavoured soup made with an assortment of leafy vegetables. It’s the perfect soup that accommodates a variety of meats as it does with vegetables! A super-versatile soup popular amongst the Efik and Ibibio people of Nigeria . This is the Ultimate guide to making an Amazing Afang /Eru soup with Spinach

    Prep time: 15 mins

    Cook time: 35 mins

    How to make White soup

    It is very popular in the south and eastern part of Nigeria, the efiks and Igbos are the top makers and consumers of this delicious soup. So if you are dating or married to a man from the south or eastern part of Nigeria, you can try giving him a meal of pounded yam and ofe nsala tonight.

    Ingredients

    • Yam (six to eight slices…baby fist size)
    • Ground crayfish (1 cup)
    • 3 seasoning cubes
    • 1 teaspoon Uziza seeds
    • 1 cup sliced uziza leaves
    • Dried or smoked fish (two medium sizes)
    • 1.3KG / 3lb Chicken
    • handful sliced utazi leaves

    Instructions

    Preparing soup;

    1. Peel and boil some yam, then pound and set aside. You need about 1KG of pounded yam.
    2. Combine crayfish, uziza seeds, dawadawa and blend to a powder.
    3. Prepare all the other ingredients (slice the leaves, wash the snails using lime or orange juice)
    4. Season the chicken with 2 stock/seasoning cubes, teaspoon each of ginger and garlic, teaspoon of salt and allow to boil then add the washed fish.
    5. Add about 3 cups of water.
    6. Bring to boil, you want the meat and fish soft and easy to chew. Then add the crayfish, uziza seed blend. The pepper should be followed after tasting.
    7. You can taste your soup. You can add an extra seasoning cube or more salt to taste
    8. Introduce the pounded yam in small portions.
    9. Let it dissolve and thicken. Then the leaves should come in.
    10. Let it simmer on low heat for few minutes and you are done.
