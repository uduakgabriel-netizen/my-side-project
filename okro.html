    Prep time: 15 mins

    Cook time: 35 mins

    How to make Afang soup

    This Nigerian Okra soup recipe is a delicious, brightly coloured medley of delectable meats, vegetables and spices. This slimy goodness is astonishingly easy to prepare and it will leave you very satisfied…right after you go back for seconds-hehe!

    Spices

    • Cameroon pepper
    • stock cubes
    • crayfish powder

    Ingredients

    • 5 cups Chopped okra
    • 1 Cup blended okra
    • 3 Red bell peppers
    • 2 Scotch bonnets
    • 2 cayenne peppers
    • 1 lb meat of choice goat meat, tripe, smoked turkey
    • 1/2 cup palm oil
    • 1 tbsp iru (locust beans)
    • spinach

    >

    Instructions

    Prepare meat;

    • Wash goat meat and tripe (or meat of choice) and place it into a pot
    • In a blender add 1 onion and 2 scotch bonnets. Blend into a paste and pour into the pot of meat
    • Season the meat with salt, cameroon pepper, paprika and 2 stock cubes. Place on medium heat and boil for 30 minutes or till soft.
    • Soak dry fish in hot water for 5 minutes to soften it

    Prep Vegetables;

    1. Blend Red bell peppers, scotch bonnet,1/2 onion and cayenne peppers and transfer to an empty pot to boil for 15 minutes( or till dry)

    Preparing soup;

    1. Add palm oil to the pot a and season with iru, crayfish and 1 stock cube
    2. Add pepper blend into oil and let it fry for 1 minutes. Then pour in the pot of meat( with stock),
    3. Taste sauce and season with salt, pepper and crayfish (to taste)
    4. Pour the blended okra into the soup and stir well. Wait a few seconds then add chopped okra and spinach into the pot
    5. Stir soup well and allow it to simmer for 10 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
