    • Rice and stew Recipe

    An assortment of meats cooked inside a rough blend of bell peppers. Nigerians like to call it Ofada or Ayamase and no matter how you call it OMOâ€¦ The enjoyment is still certain at the end of this Ofada stew recipe so Get ready to prepare a Lagos staple in the comfort of your own home.

    Prep time: 15 mins

    Cook time: 1hr 30 mins

    How to make Nigerian Rice and Stew

    Remember to adjust the recipe to suit your personal preferences. Feel free to add more spices or vegetables to enhance the flavors. Jollof Rice and Beans is a versatile dish that can be customized to your liking.

    Spices

    • Salt
    • curry
    • black pepper
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
    • 2 tsp chicken bullion
    • chilli powder
    • Crayfish

    Ingredients

    • 4 cups Parboiled rice washed
    • 2 cups black-eyed beans
    • 7 large red bell peppers
    • 4 scotch bonnets
    • 3 large plum tomatoes
    • 2-3 tbsp tomato paste
    • 1 large onion
    • 1 cup beef stock (optional)
    • Olive oil
    • 2 Chicken thighs chopped and seasoned with salt, pepper, chilli powder, soy sauce and buillion
    • 4 cups Chicken stock can be homemade or store bought

    Instructions

    Parboiing the rice

    1. heat water-In a large pot over medium heat add rice and water. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and let simmer, covered, for 45 minutes or until rice is soft and set aside

    Preparing the stew;

    1. Roast bell peppers in oven for 10 minutes, peel black skin off and Blend all the peppers into a rough paste
    2. Bleach palm oil by adding it to a pot, closing the lid, heating it up for 10-15 mins, turning off the heat and letting it cool down a bit.
    3. Once oil is warm, add in chopped onions and fry till its translucent. Season with iru and crayfish
    4. Add blended bell pepper paste to oil and let it fry for 45 minutes, stirring occasionally.
    5. Add Roasted meat to the pot along with 1/2 cup of stock, salt, Cameroon pepper, stock cubes, crayfish and thyme. Simmer for 5 minutes , then add Boiled eggs
    6. Serve hot.
