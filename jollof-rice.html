Unlike regular OG Jollof rice, One skillet Jollof rice takes 30 minutes and requires minimal clean up . There is absolutely no need to plan ahead or boil this or that ahead of time. You just need to have your ingredients and afrobeats playlist ready to go.

    Prep time: 15 mins

    Cook time: 30 mins

    How to make Jollof Rice

    I like to call this “lazy-Jollof” because it takes very little effort. if you get it right you may never want to go back to the OG method ever again!

    Spices

    • Salt
    • chilli flakes
    • black pepper
    • 1 tbsp curry powder
    • 1 bayleaf
    • 4 Stock cubes
    • thyme
    • curry
    • oregano

    Ingredients

    • 4 Chicken breasts
    • 3 1/2 cups Parboiled Rice Washed
    • 2 Red bell peppers
    • 1 scotch bonnet
    • 2 tbsp tomatoes paste
    • 1 tbsp garlic-ginger paste
    • Spring onion for garnish
    • Oil to fry

    Instructions

    Season Chicken;

    1. in a small bowl, mix salt, chilli flakes, thyme oregano, curry and 1 stock cube with oil and generously coat your chicken breast
    2. In a large skillet over medium heat, add some oil and place chicken to the pan and brown for 3 minutes on both sides. Set aside (it should be semi cooked)
    3. Clean off any burnt spices on your skillet with with spoon of any
    4. Add a little more oil to the pan to fry eggs, once they are fried and scrambled add cooled rice. Then add stir fried chicken, shrimp and veggies to the fried pot. Mix well and fry rice for 7 minutes
    5. Adjust seasoning according to your taste and add chopped spring onions to garnish
    6. Take the pot off the heat and serve.

    Prepare the rice;

    1. Add oil to the pot and sauté onion with ginger garlic paste for a few seconds then add tomatoe paste
    2. Fry tomatoe paste till it begins to darken and split.
    3. Blend bell peppers and scotch bonnet then add to the pot. Let it fry for 8 minutes till it reduces. Season with Salt, bayleaf, curry, thyme, oregano, stock cubes and chilli flakes. Stir well
    4. Add enough water to cover the rice ,reduce the heat to low, cover pan with aluminium foil let the rice steam for 10 minutes. After that , place the chicken on top the rice and let it steam for 5-8 additional minutes on low heat
    5. Fluff rice with spoon and serve.
